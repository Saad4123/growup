Our games are specially designed to harness your imagination and social self-efficacy.

VIP CAP

VIP Cap service is offered in areas of Jammu and Azad Kashmir which enables passengers to share their rides with other passengers. In the distressing times of inflation and low employment in the country, it is rational to share than to spend individually. VIP Cap offers full security with a location sharing option and the availability of complete identity information of passengers sharing the rides and the Driver as well via the application. VIP Cap service provides efficient and cost-effective rides at your doorstep providing full autonomy to the passengers in sharing options.

NUML TRANSPORT SERVICE

After explicitly observing the transport service and related problems in the NUML University Islamabad, Grow Up Tech Offers its services in booking online transport for university students. NUML transport service aims to reduce the hustle of going to transport offices for registration several times, finding whether your area is on the route or not, filling lengthy forms, long waiting, and running after the bus in case one is late. NUML Transport service provides assured comfortable and fair priced pick and drop rides to the students at any time during University hours.

INTERVIEW PREPARATION

If you are preparing for any government job related to any field of Engineering, Medical, IR, Mass Communication, Logic, and Sciences, Grow Up Tech got you all covered. Our expert team has developed an online assessment and preparatory site with realistic preparation material to help you prepare and thousands of assessment tests to evaluate your performance. Job seekers in Pakistan preparing for any educational test can have access to our website freely. Grow Up Tech will protect you from interview embarrassments/rejections by providing all requisite information via its highly formulated preparatory setup.

GARI KAR

It is an app-based vehicle booking platform under Grow Up Tech which provides highly maintained and reliable transport service in minutes. Gari Kar not only offers convenient and cost-effective rides but also provides rich opportunities for youngsters and entrepreneurs to connect with the network who are in pursuit of employment and robust business setups. Gari Kar provides exciting offers in credit transfer, business partnerships, courier services, and meal deals. It is a free application designed especially for the people of far-flung areas of Pakistan who are deprived of secure and reliable transport services.
