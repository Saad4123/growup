VIP CAP

VIP Cap service is offered in areas of Jammu and Azad Kashmir which enables passengers to share their rides with other passengers. In the distressing times of inflation and low employment in the country, it is rational to share than to spend individually. VIP Cap offers full security with a location sharing option and the availability of complete identity information of passengers sharing the rides and the Driver as well via the application. VIP Cap service provides efficient and cost-effective rides at your doorstep providing full autonomy to the passengers in sharing options.

NUML TRANSPORT SERVICE
